Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an amazing meat sauce, layered with tender noodles, ricotta cheese, and topped with melted mozzarella. It's the perfect Italian comfort food that feeds a crowd!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 large onion, minced
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 cans crushed tomatoes
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Brown the meat with onions and garlic
  2. Add tomatoes and simmer the sauce
  3. Cook lasagna noodles
  4. Layer the lasagna: sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella
  5. Repeat layers and top with remaining cheese
  6. Bake at 375Â°F for 25 minutes
