Classic Spaghetti

Description

A timeless Italian classic featuring al dente spaghetti topped with savory meatballs and rich tomato sauce. This comfort food favorite is perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans crushed tomatoes
  • Fresh basil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook spaghetti according to package instructions
  2. Form and cook meatballs until browned
  3. Prepare sauce with garlic, onions, and tomatoes
  4. Simmer meatballs in sauce
  5. Serve sauce over spaghetti
  6. Top with fresh basil and grated parmesan
